Why Choose Betweena
Built for African Commerce
Every feature designed around the realities of buying and selling in Ghana and across Africa.
🔐
Secure Escrow Vault
Funds are held in a neutral escrow account until both parties fulfil their obligations. Neither buyer nor seller can touch the money mid-transaction.
⚖️
Built-in Dispute Resolution
If something goes wrong, raise a dispute and our team steps in. Fair, fast, and impartial mediation for every case.
💬
In-Transaction Chat
Every transaction has a built-in private chat so buyer and seller can communicate directly — all messages are on record.
🌍
Multi-Currency Support
Trade in GHS, USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, or KES. Betweena converts automatically so both parties always see amounts in their preferred currency.
📦
Shipment Tracking
Sellers can attach tracking info. Buyers can verify delivery status before releasing funds. Every step is logged on the transaction timeline.
❤️
NGO Fundraising
Support verified NGOs and community causes directly from your wallet. Transparent fundraiser tracking shows exactly where your money goes.
